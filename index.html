A Note on Salad Dressings and Marinades

Make sure to use a base of an oil and acid, add anything else you want after

Basic Vinaigrette:

  • Olive Oil (Oil)
  • Red Wine Vinegar (Acid)
  • Aromat
  • Pepper
  • Mustard
  • Minced Garlic
  • Honey/Agave Syrup (Optional)

Alternative:

  • Olive Oil (Oil)
  • Balsamic Vinegar (Acid)
  • Mustard
  • Garlic/Garlic Powder
  • Aromat
  • Pepper
  • Maple Syrup (Optional)
